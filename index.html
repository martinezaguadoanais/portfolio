ABOUT ME

As the headline, I am a UX/UI designer and sociologist, currently based in Amsterdam.

I am a methodical person, who can adapt to both: lean and more structured working environments. I spend my spare time studying a degree in Psychology, which so far has helped me to understand users from multiple perspectives and has often made me resourceful in my previous works.

Thanks to my sociological background, I have developed a great ability to analyse and interpret information, which helps me to be a detail-oriented person which always keeps an open mind and takes into account the qualitative and quantitative side of the picture.

As a result of my interest and sympathy for technology, I have developed wide design capabilities that often complements my other skills when it comes to proposing solutions from different perspectives to improve the user experience.
